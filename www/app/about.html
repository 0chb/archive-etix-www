The Data Center company that's fixing Data Centers

We created Etix Labs to be different from other Data Center suppliers. Unlike the big names that over engineer and overcharge, we make a high-quality, innovative and easy to use stack of tools that’s made by smart data center builders for critical building operators. Etix Labs is focused on making simple, human oriented, delightful buildings. That’s how we are building the smart data center: using technology that takes care of the people inside and the world around it.

Labs in Luxembourg

We design and build our products in our Lab in Luxembourg, an ecosystem on the rise that is becoming a technology hub.

Agile Culture

More than Agile methodologies, the agile culture is in Etix Labs’s DNA. Fast releases, adaptability by learning from our customers, keeping things simple and live users testing. We have put in place the right environment where creativity can blossom at every step of the product development.

Going Forward

We constantly improve our way of working, developing and doing business, adding technical and human value to our products.
